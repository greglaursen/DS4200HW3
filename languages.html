My Self-Studying Language Journey

I started my language learning journey several years ago, focusing on developing conversational fluency in multiple different languages including Hebrew and Farsi. My approach is mostly self-guided through online resources, media, and conversations with native speakers. Language learning can open your eyes to new cultures and allows you to connect with people on a deeper level.

Hebrew

I've been learning Hebrew for almost 3 years now. It's a beautiful and meaningful language, but very difficult and requires consistant practice. Here are some of my favorite resources for starting out!

  • Verb Conjugation Playlist
  • Streetwise Hebrew Podcast
  • Piece of Hebrew - Youtbe Channel

Farsi

I began learning Farsi only a few months ago. It is a very interesting language and I really enjoy Persian food and culture. Here are some of the resources I've been using to learn Farsi.

  • Reading and Writing in Persian
  • Farsi Podcast
  • Pimsleur Farsi - Paid Resource
